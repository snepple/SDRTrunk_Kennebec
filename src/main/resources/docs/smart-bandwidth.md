# 🧠 Smart Bandwidth

> Let SDRTrunk Kennebec automatically optimize your antenna's power so you never miss a call!

When you set up an SDR (your USB antenna), you usually have to guess how "wide" of a radio view it needs to capture. This is called "bandwidth." If you guess too small, you'll miss calls. If you guess too big, your computer might slow down or crash.

**Smart Bandwidth** removes the guessing game!

## 📏 How it works

Think of your SDR antenna like a camera lens.
* Without Smart Bandwidth, you have to manually zoom the lens in and out.
* With Smart Bandwidth, Kennebec acts like **Autofocus**. 

It looks at all the channels you want to listen to, figures out how far apart they are from each other on the radio dial, and perfectly adjusts your antenna to cover exactly what is needed—no more, no less.

## ✨ The Benefits

* **💻 Less Computer Strain:** By only capturing exactly what is needed, your computer doesn't have to work as hard, keeping things fast and cool.
* **🔧 Zero Math:** You don't have to calculate megahertz or frequencies. Just add your channels and Kennebec handles the math behind the scenes.
* **🛡️ Increased Reliability:** Perfect bandwidth means fewer decoding errors and clearer audio.

> [!TIP]
> Smart Bandwidth is enabled automatically for most supported tuners (like RTL-SDR and Airspy)! You can adjust the maximum limits in your **User Preferences** if you want to set a "speed limit" for your computer.
