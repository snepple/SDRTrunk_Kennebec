# Guides & Documentation

Welcome to the SDRTrunk Kennebec help system. Every guide is grouped into the categories below — expand a category in the sidebar on the left, or use the **search box** at the top to jump straight to a topic.

## Not sure where to begin?

If this is your first time, follow these three guides in order:

1. **Getting Started → Introduction** — what SDRTrunk Kennebec is, explained with simple analogies.
2. **Getting Started → Quickstart** — plug in your SDR and hear your first channel, step by step.
3. **Integrations & Streaming → Overview** — send your audio to your phone or share it online.

## The categories

| Category | What you'll find |
| --- | --- |
| **Getting Started** | Orientation for brand-new users: what the app does and how to hear your first call. |
| **Hardware & Tuners** | Choosing an SDR, reading the spectrum display, and keeping tuners healthy. |
| **Channels & Decoding** | Setting up P25, DMR, and analog channels, plus audio-quality tuning. |
| **Organization & Playlists** | Building playlists, naming talkgroups with aliases, and importing systems. |
| **Integrations & Streaming** | Sending audio to Broadcastify, Zello, Rdio Scanner, paging, and automation. |
| **Advanced & System** | Installation, preferences, monitoring, and power-user features. |
