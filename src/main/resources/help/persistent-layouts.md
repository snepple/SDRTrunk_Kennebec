## Goal
Understand how SDRTrunk saves and restores your window layouts.

## Overview
Persistent Layouts automatically save the width, visibility, and ordering of your channel columns, so you don't have to reconfigure them every time you start the application.

> **Tip**
> If you frequently change environments, set up your ideal layout once and SDRTrunk will remember it across sessions.

## Process
1. Open the Channels tab in the main interface.
2. Drag column headers to reorder them or resize them to your preference.
3. Close the application. When you restart, the application will restore your saved layout.

## Component Map
* **Column Header:** Drag to resize or reorder columns.
* **Reset Layout Button:** (If applicable) Restores the default column view.

For more information on navigating the interface, see the [Overview](/help/overview.md) guide.
