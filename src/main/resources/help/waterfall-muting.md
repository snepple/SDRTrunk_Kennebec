## Goal
Mute or unmute audio directly from the spectral waterfall display.

## Overview
Waterfall Muting provides a quick shortcut to toggle audio for a specific channel without having to navigate away from the hardware tuning display.

> **Warning**
> Muting a channel on the waterfall will persist until you manually unmute it.

## Process
1. Open the Tuner Waterfall display.
2. Locate the active channel signal on the waterfall.
3. Right-click or use the designated UI button on the channel indicator to toggle the audio mute state.

## Component Map
* **Mute Toggle:** Quickly turns audio on or off for the selected signal.
* **Signal Indicator:** Represents the active channel on the waterfall.

For more information on spectral displays, see the [Spectrum & Waterfall](/help/spectrum-&-waterfall.md) guide.
